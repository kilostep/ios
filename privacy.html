KiloStep Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

KiloStep (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the KiloStep mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: identifiers you provide at sign-in (e.g. Apple ID–related tokens as processed by our authentication provider), display name, and profile details you choose to add.
  • Content you create: text, photos, audio, or other materials you upload for walks, events, chat, or your profile.
  • Usage & device data: app version, device type, OS version, coarse diagnostics, and aggregated analytics to improve stability and features.
  • Optional permissions: camera, microphone, photo library, and (where applicable) tracking—only when you grant them and for the purposes disclosed in the system permission prompts.

2. How we use information

We use data to operate the Service (accounts, walks and events, messaging, safety tools), personalize recommendations where applicable, maintain security, comply with law, and communicate important notices about KiloStep.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with infrastructure and analytics vendors under contract, when required by law, or to protect users and the Service. Content you post publicly may be visible to other users as designed in the app.

4. Retention & security

We retain information as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.

5. Your choices

You may update profile data in the app, revoke permissions in device settings, and request account deletion where offered. You may contact us using the email below to exercise applicable privacy rights.

6. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from young children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version in-app or via notice and update the effective date.
