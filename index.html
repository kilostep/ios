KiloStep Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

KiloStep ("we," "us," or "our") understands the importance of your personal information and is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use the KiloStep application and related services (the "Service"), and the rights you have. This Service is intended for users in supported regions outside restricted territories.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Information you provide during registration or sign-in, such as username, email address, and phone number.
  • Profile Information: Personal profile details you choose to provide, such as bio, profile picture, gender, and region.
  • User-Generated Content: Content you create and share through the Service, including posts, photos, videos, route logs, likes, comments, favorites, and shares.
  • Device and Log Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifier, IP address, app crash information, and high-level usage statistics.
  • Camera and Photo Library Access (with permission): When you upload photos or videos, we access these features only after your authorization.

2. How We Use This Information

We follow the principles of legality, fairness, and necessity. We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide core service functions, including publishing and displaying content, and recommending routes or users.
  • Enable community interactions such as likes, comments, follows, and private messaging.
  • Maintain service security and stability, including troubleshooting, security checks, analytics, and product optimization.
  • Send important service notifications such as policy updates and account security alerts.
  • Send product updates or promotional information, where required with your consent.

3. How We Share, Transfer, or Publicly Disclose Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

We only share information in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • For user content sharing features, where content you publish publicly is visible according to your app settings.
  • With trusted service partners (such as cloud providers or support vendors) solely for purposes in this policy, under strict confidentiality and security obligations.
  • When required by applicable law, regulation, or valid requests from competent authorities.

4. Information Storage and Security

Your personal information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions outside your country or region. Where cross-border transfers occur, we implement reasonable safeguards required under applicable law and contract to protect your data.

We use technical and administrative measures in line with industry standards, such as encrypted transmission, access controls, and security audits, to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, use, alteration, damage, or loss.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You can view and edit profile information in the app.
  • Deletion: You may delete certain content or request account deactivation; we will process deletion or anonymization according to applicable law.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can revoke camera/photo-library permissions in system settings and opt out of marketing notifications where applicable.
  • Account Deactivation: You can submit deactivation requests in app; after deactivation, we stop service and process your data as legally required.

6. Protection of Minors

We take the protection of minors' personal information seriously. If you are under 18, please use the Service under the guidance of your parent or legal guardian. For minors' information collected with guardian consent, we only use or disclose it as permitted by law, with guardian authorization, or as necessary to protect the minor.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted in app or through other appropriate channels, and the effective date will be updated. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.
